### LEAVE POLICY

Sick Leave (SL)
- Full-time employees receive 12 days of paid sick leave per year.
- Sick leave is pro-rated for employees who join mid-year.
- Cannot be encashed.
- A medical certificate may be needed if sick leave exceeds 2 consecutive days.
- If you feel unwell (fever, cold, headache), you may use sick leave.
- To apply: Inform your manager → HR Portal → Leave → Sick Leave → Submit.

Casual Leave (CL)
- Employees receive 10 casual leave days per year.
- Cannot be carried forward.
- Used for personal work, small emergencies, or urgent tasks.
- To apply: HR Portal → Leave → Casual Leave.

Earned Leave (EL)
- Earned at 1.5 days per month of service.
- Can be accumulated up to 30 days.
- Extra days beyond 30 may be eligible for encashment.
- Often used for vacations or long breaks.
- To apply: HR Portal → Leave → Earned Leave.

Maternity Leave
- 26 weeks of paid maternity leave.
- Can be taken before or after delivery.
- Extension may be requested with medical documents and HR approval.

Paternity Leave
- 7 days of paid paternity leave.
- Should be used within 3 months of childbirth.

Half-Day Leave
- Allowed for short personal tasks or health issues.
- Apply as half-day casual or sick leave as appropriate.


### LEAVE BALANCE

### HOW TO APPLY FOR LEAVE

General Steps
- Inform your manager politely.
- Open HR Portal → Leave.
- Select the correct leave type.
- Choose the dates.
- Add a short, polite reason.
- Submit for approval.

Examples of polite reasons:
- “Not feeling well today.”
- “Family errand to handle.”
- “Doctor appointment.”
- “Attending a family function.”

### GENERAL OFFICE POLICIES

Code of Conduct
- Maintain professionalism and respect colleagues.
- Bullying, harassment, or discrimination is strictly not allowed.
- Confidential company info must not be shared externally.

Dress Code
- Smart casual from Monday to Friday.
- Casual wear allowed on Fridays.
- Avoid inappropriate or offensive prints.

Clean Desk Policy
- Keep your workspace tidy.
- Do not leave sensitive documents open.
- Lock your screen before leaving desk.

Security & Access
- Always wear your ID card inside the office.
- Visitors need prior approval.
- Do not share access cards.

Food & Beverages
- Food allowed in pantry areas.
- Eating at desks is fine if cleanliness is maintained.
- Alcohol is not permitted on office premises.

Transport Policy
- Late-night employees may use company-arranged cabs.
- Travel misuse may lead to disciplinary action.


### TECHNOLOGY & IT POLICIES

Password Security
- Update passwords every 90 days.
- Never share login credentials.

Email Policy
- Use company email for work.
- Avoid opening unverified attachments.
- Sensitive data must be encrypted.

Data Privacy
- Client and company data must be handled carefully.

Software Usage
- Only IT-approved software allowed.

Internet Use
- Internet must be used responsibly.
